Mahmoud El Khatib , born 30 October 1954, popularly nicknamed Bibo (Arabic: Bebo), is an Egyptian retired footballer and current President of Al Ahly Sporting Club. He is widely regarded as one of the best players in African football history. On December 1, 2017, he was elected president of the Al-Ahly club, succeeding engineer Mahmoud Taher after obtaining 20,956 votes
